About Sodalite

Selenite is a unblocked games website with over 250 games, with a simple tab cloak. Selenite was started as a small project shared between friends. After ~1 month, she transferred to a custom made website. She abandoed the static selenite a few year ago and I used that as the base for the Sodalite(unless you work at a school).

I belive in project based learning and saw a great opportunity to improve my web dev skills by starting a games site.
