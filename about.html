About Selenite

Selenite is a unblocked games website with over 200 games, with a simple tab cloak. Selenite was started as a small project started on Padlet, shared between friends. After ~1 month, they transferred to a custom made website. After a few rebrandings and redesigns, they ended up at the Selenite you know and love (unless you work at a school). I now maintain and continue update the site.
